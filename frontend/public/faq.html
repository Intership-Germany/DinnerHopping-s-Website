Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DinnerHopping

DinnerHopping is a unique student dinner event where you get to meet new people while enjoying a three-course meal. You'll visit three different locations throughout the evening - one for the starter, one for the main course, and one for dessert. At each location, you'll be matched with different groups of students, giving you the opportunity to make new friends and have interesting conversations.

To register for an event, first create an account on our platform by clicking "Log In" and then "Sign Up". Once your account is created and verified, navigate to the Events page where you can browse upcoming DinnerHopping events. Select an event that interests you and complete the registration form. You can register as an individual or as part of a team.

Team registration allows you to sign up with a friend or partner. When registering, select the "Team Registration" option and provide your partner's email address. They will receive an invitation to join your team. Both team members need to complete their registration and payment. Teams stay together for the first course but will be split up for the remaining courses to maximize networking opportunities.

If your team partner cancels, don't worry! You can choose to continue participating as an individual, and we'll match you with other participants. Alternatively, you can invite a new partner to take their place. If you decide to cancel as well, please do so as early as possible to help us with planning. Cancellation policies depend on how far in advance you notify us - check your event confirmation email for specific details.

Payment is processed securely online after you complete your registration. We accept PayPal and credit/debit cards via Stripe. The €10 registration fee covers the organizational costs, event coordination, matching algorithm, and support throughout the evening. Please note that this fee does not include the cost of food and drinks at the venues - participants are responsible for their own meals at each location.

To modify your account information, log in and navigate to your Profile page. Here you can update your personal details, dietary restrictions, and preferences. If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team through the Contact page. We'll process your request and ensure all your personal data is removed in accordance with GDPR regulations.

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can reach our support team through the Contact page. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters on event days, emergency contact information will be provided in your event confirmation email.

We strive to accommodate various dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and common allergies. During registration, you'll have the opportunity to specify your dietary needs. We work with our partner venues to ensure they can accommodate these requirements. However, please note that while we do our best, we cannot guarantee all venues will have extensive options for every restriction.

DinnerHopping events are primarily designed for students and young professionals. While we focus on creating a student-friendly atmosphere, recent graduates and young professionals are also welcome to join. Please check the specific event requirements during registration.

Bring your confirmation email (digital or printed), a valid ID, payment method for food and drinks at venues, and most importantly, bring your enthusiasm and openness to meet new people! We also recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes if venues are spread across the city.
